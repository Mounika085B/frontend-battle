The NeuralFlow Lifecycle

From raw data to production
in four steps.

  1. 01

    Connect Your Sources

    Point NeuralFlow at any data source — databases, SaaS APIs, event streams, or file systems — via 200+ pre-built connectors or a custom SDK.

  2. 02

    Design Your Pipeline

    Compose transform, filter, enrich, and branch operations using our visual builder or infrastructure-as-code YAML.

  3. 03

    Train & Register Models

    Trigger managed training runs, track experiments, and promote champion models directly to the global model registry.

    Deploy & Observe
